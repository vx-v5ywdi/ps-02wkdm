Nearly 6,000 counterfeit branded goods seized in a GDCOC operation in Stara Zagora

12.06.2026Counterfeiting

Three people charged under Articles 172b and 321 of the Criminal Code

Photo: GDCOC

Nearly 6,000 items bearing various protected trademarks were seized during a GDCOC police operation in Stara Zagora on 9 June 2026.

During the search of a premises, officers found over 5,500 perfumes, more than 450 perfume testers and over 20 textile products. Pre-trial proceedings were opened under the supervision of the Stara Zagora District Prosecutor’s Office.

Three people were charged under Articles 321(3) and 172b of the Criminal Code — for trading in a trademark and products without the consent of the holder of the exclusive right.
